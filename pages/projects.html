sptobuff

An app that compares Counter Strike weapon skin prices from marketplaces "Buff.163" and "Skinport".

Background info:
Buff usually has cheaper prices, then most markets. So having Buff balance is important. But depositing money on Buff is difficult since you need a Chinese residency. So with this tool, you can find cheaper skins on Skinport and then sell them on Buff, for a slight profit along with balance.

languages: python
